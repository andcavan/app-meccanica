# Fonti tecniche TCEI / TSEI (2026-02-13)

Riferimenti principali usati per impostare le norme di riferimento in anagrafica:

- ISO 4762 (Socket head cap screws): https://www.iso.org/standard/78387.html
- ISO 10642 (Hexagon socket countersunk head screws): https://www.iso.org/standard/78401.html
- ISO 898-1 (Mechanical properties of carbon steel/alloy steel fasteners): https://www.iso.org/standard/66154.html
- ISO 4042 (Electroplated coating systems for fasteners): https://www.iso.org/standard/77849.html
- ISO 3506-1 (Mechanical properties of corrosion-resistant stainless-steel fasteners): https://www.iso.org/standard/70150.html

## Note operative

- Gerarchia applicata nelle note e descrizioni: `ISO > UNI > DIN`.
- In descrizione la norma viene inserita prima della dimensione metrica `M`.
- Le equivalenze `DIN 912` (per TCEI) e `DIN 7991` (per TSEI) sono mantenute come livello DIN nella gerarchia interna.
